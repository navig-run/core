# LifeOS Pack

A comprehensive suite for managing your personal life with privacy-focused, local-first tools.

## Included Skills

### 1. `fin-ledger` (Finance)
Plaintext accounting for bulletproof financial tracking.
- `finance.record`: Log expenses instantly.
- `finance.balance`: Check your net worth.

### 2. `task-warrior` (Productivity)
CLI-based task management.
- `task add`: Capture to-dos.
- `task next`: See what to work on now.

### 3. `knowledge-md` (Second Brain)
Markdown-based knowledge base.
- `know.capture`: Quick notes.
- `know.search`: Recall information.

## Daily Routine Template
1. **Morning**: `task next` to pick 3 priorities.
2. **Review**: `know.search` for relevant project context.
3. **Log**: `finance.record` lunch expense suitable for billing.
4. **Evening**: `task done` to close loops.
